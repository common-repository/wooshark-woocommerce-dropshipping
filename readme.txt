Dropshipping and affiliates for Amazon and woocommerce Allows to import products from Amazon to your woocommerce wordpress store.
 
== Description ==

Dropshipping and affiliates for Amazon and woocommerce allows to import and sell Amazon products on your WooCommerce-wordpress store with just 1 click. 
With the WooShark app, you get better sales and increased overall productivity, 
while reducing the time and effort invested to transfer your products to your online store.

==  FEATURES ==  
 
+ Import Amazon product details by URL
including 
+ product images 
+ product Title
+ product weight
+ product price
+ product description
+ product quantity
+ publish/draft a product
